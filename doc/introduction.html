Introducing Open Meta-Analyst

Open Meta-Analyst is software for performing meta-analyses, involving binary, continuous, or diagnostic data. Using Open Meta-Analyst, you can perform analyses using a variety fixed or random-effects models. You can also do cumulative, leave-one-out, and meta-regression analyses.

Installing Open Meta-Analyst

  1. Click one of the links below to download a zip file containing the software.
