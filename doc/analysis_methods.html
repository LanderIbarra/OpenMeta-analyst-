Fixed-Effect Methods

The following fixed-effect methods are available for performing a meta-analysis. You can select a method in the analysis method field in the Method & Parameters dialog.

  • Fixed-effect inverse variance
  • Mantel-Haenszel
  • Fixed-effect Peto
