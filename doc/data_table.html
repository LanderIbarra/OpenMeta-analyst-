Using the Data Table

This section describes some of the features of the Open Meta-Analyst data table.

Sorting Data by Study Name or Year

To sort your data alphabetically by study name, click study name.

To put the data back in its original order, select undo from the Edit menu. Similarly, you can sort your data chronologically by clicking year.

Note: After you sort data, when you close Open Meta-Analyst, you are prompted to save your changes. If you want to keep your data in its original (unsorted) order, click Discard in the dialog, as shown below.

If you want to save the sorted data in the new order, click Save.
